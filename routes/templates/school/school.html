PORTRAITURE (BEGINNERS)

SYLLABUS

(Main Course)

Week 1 (Fundamentals)

  • Basics of Sketching
  • Introduction to Facial Proportions
  • Loomis Method
  • Head drawing from different viewing angles
  • Grid Drawing

Week 2 (Eyes & Eyebrows)

  • Constructing eyeball, eyelids and eyelashes
  • Surrounding shadows and form
  • Drawing from different viewing angles

Week 3 (Nose & Ears)

  • Basic anatomy and form of nose
  • Basic anatomy of ears
  • Drawing both from different viewing angles

Week 4 (Mouth & Lips)

  • Anatomy and form of lips, the philtrum, the chin and the jawline
  • Drawing teeth
  • Drawing from different viewing angles

Week 5 (Hair & Skin texture)

  • Hair as mass
  • Common hair lock pattern study
  • Rendering realistic skin textures using different shading methods

Week 6 (Planes of Face)

  • Basic anatomy and form of face
  • Assembling all the already learn facial parts on face

Week 7 (Value and Contrast)

  • The 70-30 rule of values
  • Basics of value and lighting

Week 8 (Portrait Lighting)

  • Low-key and high-key lighting
  • Lighting quality (hard vs soft)
  • Classic lighting patterns

Week 9 & 10 (Expressions)

  • Happy, sad, surprised, scared, smiling, laughing, frowning, disgust etc.

Week 11 (Male vs Female vs Baby face)

  • Masculine features
  • Feminine features
  • Baby features
  • Ageing

Week 12 (History)

  • Classical portraits by the maestros

(Optional)

Week 13 to 16 (Practice, feedback & guidance)

  • Practicing portraits applying everything learnt in the previous classes
  • Feedback and guidance by the instructor on drawings by the students

REQUIREMENTS

    Pencil (Graphite or Charcoal)
    Eraser & Sharpener
    Loose paper or a Drawing pad
    Or digital drawing pad
