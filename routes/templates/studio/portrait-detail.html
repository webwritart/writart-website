Insurance:
We care for our customers' hard-earned money. Therefore, we provide an option for insurance of the Painting while in courier transit.
What does it mean? In case the painting gets damaged or gets lost and the courier provider is found responsible for the same, we help recover 100% of the total money the customer paid us for the painting using the courier insurance. As already stated, the courier cost is completely paid by us inside India but if the customer chooses to get insurance done, then the insurance charge has to be borne by the customer.

The insurance charge is transparent and is fixed. For the painting commissions amounting upto ₹20,000 the insurance charge is 1% of the total amount (for example, for a painting costing ₹18,000 total, the insurance charge will be a flat ₹180), for the painting commissions amounting ₹20,001-₹45,000 the insurance charge is 2% of the total amount and for the painting commissions amounting ₹45,001-₹50,000 the insurance charge is 2.25% of the total amount.
The refunded amount will be credited straight to the bank account provided by the customer.
